Powerful Features

ExamGenie offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed to streamline the educational assessment process.

Generate Question Papers

Create customized question papers by selecting subjects, topics, and marks. Cover all key areas of the syllabus with varied difficulty levels to ensure comprehensive assessments.

Insert New Questions

Add new questions to your question bank, categorized by subject, topic, and difficulty level for future assessments. Build a robust repository of questions over time.

Generate from Notes

Transform study materials into question papers. Our AI extracts important topics and converts them into meaningful questions, saving hours of manual question creation.

Timetable Management

Create, modify, and manage exam timetables. Set reminders and allocate time slots for different subjects to organize your assessment schedule efficiently.

Generate Answers

Efficiently generate well-explained answers for question papers, helping students review and understand solutions. Perfect for creating comprehensive study materials.

Conduct Real-Time Quizzes

Create interactive quizzes with instant feedback. Track student performance with automated evaluation and analytics to identify areas for improvement.

Quick Notes

Capture and organize thoughts on the go. Create multiple files for different subjects, classes, or topics, all auto-saved for easy access and reference.

Plagiarism Checker

Ensure originality in academic work by scanning documents against a vast database of sources. Maintain academic integrity in all assessments.

Saved Question Papers

Access previously saved question papers. View and delete your collection of generated papers for efficient resource management and reuse.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ExamGenie.

Is ExamGenie free to use?

We offer a free plan with limited features, and premium plans for advanced users who need additional functionality and higher usage limits.

Can I customize the generated question papers?

Yes! You can edit and format the paper according to your needs before downloading. Add, remove, or modify questions as needed.

How accurate is the AI-generated content?

Our AI is trained on educational content and curriculum standards to ensure high accuracy. However, we recommend reviewing the generated content before use, especially for specialized subjects.

Can I share my question papers with colleagues?

Yes, premium plans include collaboration features that allow you to share resources with other teachers at your institution.
